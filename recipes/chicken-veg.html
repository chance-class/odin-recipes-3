Chicken Vegetable Casserole

Author: Jennifer Banz, via Low Carb with Jennifer

Ingredients

  • 3 cups steamed fresh broccoli florets
  • 3 cups steamed fresh cauliflower florets
  • 1 tsp kosher salt, divided
  • 3/4 cup *half-and-half*
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 cups cooked shredded chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Whisk together the *half-and-half*, cream cheese, and the seasonings.
  3. Combine the shredded chicken, steamed broccoli and cauliflower, and the shredded carrot.
    Pour the sauce over top and stir well. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the top.
  4. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.
